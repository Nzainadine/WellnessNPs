What it is

Wellness, delivered efficiently

IV therapy delivers fluids, vitamins, minerals and antioxidants directly into your bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system for fast, complete absorption. It's a simple, relaxing way to rehydrate and replenish — whether you're recovering, run-down, or simply investing in how you feel.

Each infusion is selected to your needs and administered by licensed clinicians. We'll recommend a blend based on your goals, and review it with you before you begin.

Questions

IV therapy FAQ

Does it hurt?
Most people feel only a brief pinch when the IV is placed, then relax comfortably for the rest of the session.
How often should I do it?
It depends on your goals. Some come occasionally for a reset; others on a regular schedule. Your clinician will advise what makes sense for you.
Is IV therapy safe?
When administered by licensed clinicians after appropriate screening, IV therapy is generally well tolerated. We review your history first to confirm it's appropriate.

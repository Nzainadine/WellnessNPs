What it is

Care that finally has time for you

Too many women are told their symptoms are "normal," "just stress," or "just your age" — and sent on their way in seven minutes. Concierge medicine flips that. As a member, you get longer visits, the same provider every time, and the ability to message that provider directly between appointments.

Our focus is the hormonal arc of a woman's life — especially perimenopause and menopause — addressed with comprehensive labs and a plan that treats the whole person, not a single number. When it fits your goals, we integrate hormone therapy, weight and metabolic support, peptides and IV therapy under one roof.

Membership

Our membership model

One annual fee covers all of the time, access, services and advice our office provides over the course of 12 months.

  • Your initial / annual visit is scheduled for 2 hours; follow-up visits are typically 30–60 minutes in length.
  • Enrolled patients have access to a HIPAA-compliant phone app that allows for easy, direct text and phone communication with our office.

A $250 enrollment deposit is due at the time of your scheduled appointment. This fee is applied toward your Annual Fee and is refundable unless cancellation occurs with less than 48 hours' notice.

Questions

Concierge care FAQ

What is concierge medicine?
A membership-based model offering longer, unrushed visits, the same provider each time, and direct access between appointments — care designed around you.
Do you only see women?
Women's health is our specialty, with deep focus on perimenopause and menopause. We also care for men seeking hormone and wellness support.
How does membership work?
One annual fee covers your visits, services and direct access for 12 months. A $250 enrollment deposit is collected at your first appointment and applied toward your annual fee (refundable unless you cancel with less than 48 hours' notice).
Are visits in person or virtual?
Both. Many visits can be virtual, with in-person options available. We'll confirm what's appropriate and which states we're licensed to treat in.

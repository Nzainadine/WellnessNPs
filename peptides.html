Small signals,

Peptides are your body's own messengers. Used thoughtfully and under medical supervision, targeted protocols can support recovery, metabolism, sleep and the way you age.

What it is

Targeted, personalized protocols

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that act as signaling molecules — telling cells what to do. Different peptides support different goals: some are studied for tissue recovery and repair, others for growth-hormone support, metabolism, sleep quality or immune function.

Rather than a generic stack, we build a protocol around your goals and labs, then prescribe and monitor it under medical supervision. We're also candid about the evidence — what's well-studied, what's emerging, and what we'd recommend for you.

Questions

Peptide FAQ

Are peptides safe?
When prescribed and monitored by a licensed provider, peptides are used thoughtfully and individualized to you. We review benefits, limitations and any risks before you begin, and monitor you throughout.
Are peptides FDA-approved?
Many peptides used in wellness are not FDA-approved for the purposes discussed. We're transparent about this, prescribe responsibly under medical supervision, and never make cure claims.
How are peptides taken?
Depending on the peptide, they may be given as a small subcutaneous injection or other forms. Your provider explains exactly how to use your specific protocol.

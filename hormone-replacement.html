What it is

Hormones, dosed to you

Hormone replacement therapy restores hormones that decline or fall out of balance with age, stress or life stage. We use bioidentical hormones — chemically identical to the ones your body makes — and dose them to your individual labs and symptoms, not a generic template.

We care for both women and men: estrogen, progesterone and testosterone support, thyroid considerations, and more. Every plan is guided by comprehensive testing and adjusted over time, because balance is a moving target, not a one-time fix.

Questions

Hormone therapy FAQ

What is bioidentical hormone therapy?
BHRT uses hormones chemically identical to those your body produces, dosed to your individual labs and symptoms and monitored by a licensed provider.
Is HRT for women only?
No. We provide hormone optimization for both women and men, including testosterone therapy for men with appropriate symptoms and labs.
How long until I notice a difference?
It varies. Some changes appear within weeks; others take longer as levels stabilize. Your provider sets expectations and adjusts based on follow-up labs and how you feel.
Are there risks?
As with any therapy, hormone treatment has potential risks and benefits that depend on your health profile. Your provider reviews these with you so you can make an informed decision.

Questions

Good to know

What does "concierge" mean here?
It means time and access. You see the same provider each visit, your appointments aren't rushed, and you can message your provider directly between visits through a HIPAA-secure app.
Do you offer virtual visits?
Yes. Many visits can be done virtually, with in-person options available. We'll confirm what's appropriate for your care and which states we're licensed to treat in during your consult.
How do I get started?
Schedule a consultation. We'll review your symptoms, health history and goals, then order any labs needed to build your personalized plan.
Is treatment right for me?
That's exactly what your consultation is for. Eligibility for any therapy is determined individually by a licensed provider based on your history, labs and goals.
